N5 Self Evaluation List:

## Speaking
- [ ] Join in conversations on familiar everyday topics (e.g. hobbies, weekend plans, etc.)
- [ ] Ask when is convenient for the other person and decide a day and time to meet
- [ ] Express feelings such as surprise and joy, and the reasons for them
- [ ] Describe my room.
- [ ] My hobbies and interests.
- [ ] Simple communication using phrases commonly used at stores, post offices, and stations (e.g. “How much is it?” “Can I have ○○?”,etc.)
- [ ] Introduce myself and answer simple questions about myself

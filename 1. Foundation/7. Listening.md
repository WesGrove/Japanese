N5 Self Evaluation List:

## Listening
- [ ] Simple self-introductions by teachers and friends in classrooms
- [ ] Common phrases used at stores, post offices, and stations (e.g.“May I help you?” “It’s ○○yen.” and “Here you are.”, etc.)
- [ ] Instructions from my teacher such as meeting times and places
- [ ] Understand requirement from simple instructions
- [ ] Conversations on familiar everyday topics (e.g. hobbies, food, weekend plans, etc.)
- [ ] Basic walking directions and directions for public transportation
- [ ] Understand the general content when I engage in small talk and free conversations with people around me.
